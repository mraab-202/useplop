FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What is Plop?

Plop is a modern, custom-branded opportunities board built for university entrepreneurship centers. It helps students discover internships, jobs, competitions, grants, and accelerators all in one place. Each center gets its own branded subdomain (e.g., youruniversity.useplop.com) with their logo and colors.

Who is Plop for?

Plop is designed for university entrepreneurship centers, innovation hubs, incubators, and accelerators that want to share opportunities — internships, jobs, competitions, grants, and accelerator programs — with their students.

How much does Plop cost?

Plop is free for the first 30 days — no credit card required. After that, it’s $25 per month or $250 per year (saving $50). This includes everything: custom branding, email subscriptions, admin dashboard, category filters, mobile-friendly design, and unlimited posts. No hidden fees or tiered plans.

How long does it take to set up?

Less than 2 minutes. Sign up, add your logo and colors, and share your custom URL with your community. Opportunities can start flowing in immediately.

What types of opportunities can be posted?

Plop supports categories including internships, jobs, competitions, grants, and accelerators. Anyone can submit an opportunity, and admins approve submissions with one click before they go live on the board.

Can students get notified about new opportunities?

Yes. Students can subscribe to email notifications and get alerted whenever new opportunities are posted to the board, so they never miss a deadline.
