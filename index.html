Features

Everything your center needs.

One board. Zero friction. All the opportunities your students care about.

Custom-Branded Board

Your logo, your colors, your subdomain. Plop becomes invisible — it just looks like your site.

Email Subscriptions

Students subscribe and get notified when new opportunities drop. No more missed deadlines.

Easy Posting

Anyone can submit an opportunity. Admins approve with one click. Simple as that.

Category Filters

Internships, jobs, startup competitions — students find exactly what they’re looking for.

Mobile-First

Looks great on every device. Students browse opportunities from their phones between classes.

Manage posts, review submissions, and control everything from a simple admin panel.
