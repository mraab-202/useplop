Privacy Policy for Squash the Beef

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Squash the Beef ("we", "us", the "App") helps friends make things right after a disagreement by letting the wronged party name an amount and the offender pay to resolve it. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

Who we are

The App is operated by E is for E Ventures LLC, an Illinois limited liability company, doing business as Squash the Beef ("operator"). You can reach us at support@squashthebeef.net.

What we collect

We collect the minimum personal data needed to run the App.

We do not collect: location, browsing history outside the App, health data, photos, microphone or camera input, or your full contact list.

How we use it

  • Provide the service — show your active requests, notify the right parties, process payments, transfer funds.
  • Prevent abuse and fraud — detect and block account misuse.
  • Communicate with you — transactional SMS (via Twilio) and push notifications (via APNs) about your requests. We do not send marketing messages.

We do not sell your data. We do not use it to train advertising profiles.

Where we store it

Our backend runs on Railway in the United States. Payment data is held by Stripe under their own security and compliance program (PCI DSS Level 1).

How long we keep it

  • Active account data is retained while your account exists.
  • Resolved payment records are retained for 7 years for accounting and dispute-resolution purposes.
  • You may request deletion at any time (see below); we will delete or anonymize data not required for tax, legal, or dispute-resolution obligations.

Your rights

You can:

  • Access a copy of your data — email us.
  • Correct inaccurate data — edit in the App or email us.
  • Delete your account and associated personal data — use the in-app delete option or email us.
  • Withdraw consent for SMS or push — disable in OS settings or reply STOP to any SMS.

If you are in the EU, UK, or California, you have additional rights under GDPR/CCPA. Email us to exercise them.

Children

The App is not intended for anyone under 17, and we do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has signed up, please contact us so we can remove the account.

Security

We use TLS in transit and authenticated access controls at rest. No system is perfect. If you discover a vulnerability, please email us directly and we will respond.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be surfaced in the App. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
